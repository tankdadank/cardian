# Cardian Wiki

Welcome to the Cardian game wiki. This documentation covers all available games, their rules, variants, and how Custom mode works.

## Games

### Shipped Games (Available in all modes)

- [Blackjack](blackjack.md) - Classic dealer game with Debt variant
- [Poker](poker.md) - Texas Hold'em with Pressure variant
- [Pyramid](pyramid.md) - Multiplayer solitaire card matching

### Experimental Games (Patreon Only)

These games require the Patreon role and are only available in Casual and Custom modes.

- [Gin Rummy](rummy.md) - 2-player card melding game
- [Mao](mao.md) - Card shedding with explicit rule triggers

## Modes

### Casual
- No stakes, no ratings
- All games and variants available
- AI personalities available

### Ranked
- Affects your rating
- Monthly seasons with resets
- Only shipped games with stable variants
- No AI opponent selection

### Custom
- Choose game, variant, and modifiers
- Save presets for quick replay
- Not ranked

### SVS (Server vs Server)
- Represent your server
- Earn SVS points
- Monthly seasons
- Only shipped games
